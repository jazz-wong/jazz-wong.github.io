HOW TO FALL IN LOVE

1. Find a cute stranger to play with.

2. Assign one partner the WASD controls and the other the Arrow Keys.

3. Take turns asking and answering each question. The process can be more effective if you look into each other's eyes.

4. Together, hit the corresponding directional keys to flip through the questions. [ "A" + "<-" for the previous question, "D" + "->" for the next ]

5. Leave the rest to science.

ONEIRONAUNTS

Background

Oneironauts are a species of aliens who inhabit the subconscious through their own dreams. As of now, it is unknown how these subspaces are created or the extent of its power and ability. They survive through three hour periods of sleep walking, in which the body performs necessary functions, like consumption and excrement. While in their dream-states, Oneironauts simply stand or sit, completely stiff and frozen. This is especially easy with Luna's icy cold tempertures within its mantle. Not too much is known about these creatures, as their habitat is too deep for any of S.T.A.R.X's technology to dig deep enough. However due to the STARMAN incident of April 1990, the Xenology department has been able to capture one. Though, contact has been slim due to its current hostage.

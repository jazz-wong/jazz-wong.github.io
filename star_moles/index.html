★ STAR MOLES ★

It is said that moles are a symbol of love, physical reminders of your soulmate's kisses from past lives.
I show love for my precursor inspirations and ideas through interweaving them into my work.

★

Juxtaposition

When given a prompt, I like to imagine a contradictory concept to express it, like altering analog into digital, films into books, or retro into modern. I find humor in the artistic irony.
★

Surrealism

Merging the unconscious and reality together showcases the magical beauty hidden in the uncanny and weird. It continues to violates boundaries and finds comfort in unpredictability.
★

Vernacular World

Boundaries of the ordinary are needed in order to explore the extraordinary. The small, “default” designs in life that always get overlooked impact the way we interact with the world. They serve as a great base in order to build wild design on.
★

Film

Above all, I believe design is a form of storytelling, and film is the one of the most immersive ways to convey feeling and narrative. Borrowing colors, composition, and imagery, helps guide the story I want to tell.
★

PS2 Ads

The 2000s was an era of exploration in the grungy, thriller, and raunchy for video game advertising. These commercializations explored larger than life worlds that directly transport the consumer to in-game universes.
★

Editorialism

I am wildly inspired by indie magazines with conceptual approaches that deviate from the norm.
★

The Moon

I love the moon and all she symbolizes. Femininity. Romanization. Mystery.
